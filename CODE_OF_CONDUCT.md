# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make `claude-code-up` a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone — regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, race, religion, or anything else.

## Our standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment**:
- Concrete, kind, specific feedback ("this MCP entry doesn't have a `description`, here's the format" rather than "this is wrong")
- Crediting prior work — both in the catalog (via `mappedFrom` and `reason` fields) and in discussions
- Welcoming new contributors — answering even basic questions patiently
- Pointing at the relevant docs / file path instead of expecting people to dig

**Examples of unacceptable behavior**:
- Personal attacks, insults, demeaning comments
- Harassment, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Drive-by criticism without offering a fix or alternative

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, PRs, Discussions, and any community channels (Discord, Slack, etc.) tagged with the project.

## Enforcement

If you see something that violates this, open a private issue or email the maintainer. The maintainer will review and respond within 7 days. Outcomes range from a private nudge to a ban, depending on the situation.
